This is the official web page for Fun With Calculus, a horror object show created by parfaitheart. It is currently under construction and very barebones, so be sure to check back later!

About

Fun With Calculus is a Battle For Dream Island inspired animated series in which twenty two contestants arrive on a tropical island to battle to win a grand prize, but things quickly go wrong. To view the full series, click here!
